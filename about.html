About Mark

Mark Cayunda is a co-founder of Curve an independent art studio based in SOCCSKSARGEN that transcends the brilliance of Mindanaon creatives in different aspects of art. He is a multidisciplinary artist currently taking up Bachelor of Multimedia Arts at Systems Technology Institute of General Santos City. His interests span from visual art to literature. He is a visionary and an ardent believer of the idea that art transforms and transcends human consciousness. He nabbed several commendations in the various spectrums of media during his heyday, specifically in the field of visual journalism and page design. He was once a qualifier for the National Schools Press Conference in Pangasinan. A former EIC of two most notable campus papers in South Cotabato—in which under his time, he managed to lead the papers to win the Best in Layout and Page Design during RSPC 2019 and represented the region in NSPC 2020. Recently, one of his designed papers was hailed as the Best in Layout and Page Design during NSPC 2023 and was declared as one of the Best School Paper in the Philippines.
